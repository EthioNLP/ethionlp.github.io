Call for Papers

EthioNLP is organizing the first workshop on Natural Language Processing for Ethiopian Languages. This event focuses on the development and collaboration in NLP for Ethiopian languages. We invite researchers to submit their work on various topics related to Ethiopian languages.

Aims of the Workshop:

  • To bring global attention to Ethiopian language research
  • To support ongoing studies and mentor students in NLP

Topics of Interest Include (but not limited to):

  • Challenges or solutions for resource gathering for Ethiopian languages
  • Development of NLP systems for Ethiopian languages

Author Guidelines

Authors should prepare their manuscripts according to standard scientific paper formats. Full papers can be between 4-8 pages, and extended abstracts up to 2 pages. All submissions should be original and not simultaneously submitted to another conference.

Submission Guidelines

Papers should be formatted according to the conference style guidelines and submitted in PDF format. Ensure to remove any identifying information for blind review. Full papers and extended abstracts should be submitted through the CMT system.
