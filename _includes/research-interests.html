Research Interests

NLP corpus collection

A corpus is a fundamental requirement for NLP tasks as it comprises a collection of texts, images, audio, or video used for training and analysis. These collections can consist of content from a single language or multiple languages, depending on the task at hand. Corpora serve as the primary source of data for NLP algorithms, enabling systems to learn language patterns and semantics. The selection and curation of diverse and high-quality corpora are crucial for developing accurate and robust NLP models, allowing researchers to gain insights and advancements in language processing.

Model building

EthioNLP specializes in creating NLP and ML models specifically for Ethiopian languages. The focus is on promoting accessibility by publishing these models under lenient licenses, encouraging their utilization in a wide range of AI applications. By providing language-specific models, EthioNLP addresses the unique linguistic challenges of Ethiopian languages and contributes to advancements in language processing. Through their efforts, EthioNLP aims to foster collaboration, innovation, and the democratization of AI in Ethiopia, empowering individuals and organizations to leverage these models for language-centric tasks and supporting the growth and development of Ethiopian languages in the digital age.

Research and collaboration

EthioNLP's main objective is to establish a research platform that encourages collaborative research among scholars working on different Ethiopian languages. Recognizing the existing fragmentation in research efforts, EthioNLP aims to provide a unified space where researchers can connect, collaborate, and share their findings. By facilitating collaboration and knowledge exchange, EthioNLP seeks to strengthen the collective understanding and advancements in Ethiopian language research. Through this platform, researchers from diverse backgrounds can come together, fostering a more cohesive and synergistic research community focused on Ethiopian languages.
