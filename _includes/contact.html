How can I collaborate with EthioNLP?!

Interested in researching Ethiopian languages?

Anyone interested in NLP research for Ethiopian languages is welcome to join the group. Members are expected to contribute to the group's research and attend meetings regularly. Members are also encouraged to share their research findings and participate in group discussions.

Please visit Join Us page and join to our group mail list, Telegram or Discord group. After you join us, we will share to you two documents, Community membership rules and Research guidlines
