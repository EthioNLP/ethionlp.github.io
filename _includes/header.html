Ethio NLP

There are efforts throughout the world to conduct NLP for Ethiopian languages but without formal communication among researchers. At COLING 2018, Santa Fe, USA, three researchers from 1) Addis Ababa University (Binyam), 2) the University of Hamburg (Seid), and 3) University of Trento (Surafel) took an initiative to create a formal NLP society for Ethiopian language NLP research.
